Guitar

The guitar was my first instrument ever.

My father had advised me to learn it as it would be of much benefit in the future.

At the time I was not intersted at all and I did not want to learn it.

One day someone showed me a video of a young guitarist named Sungha Jung.

And instantly it made a strong impression on my younger self.

I began to practice and teach myself through various videos and this is how I began to play.

Due to the busy college student life style I haven't had much time to learn but I like to pick it up from time to time.
