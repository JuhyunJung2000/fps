My story

Born in South Korea on the 16th of Novemeber 2000, I grew up in a small suburb located in the city of Daejeon.

A time came when my father decided that he wanted a change for our family and he moved to Ireland to study english.

A year passsed and due to many reasons my mother, my brother and I also flew across the world to join my father.

I was about 8 years old at the time.

We soon became adapt to Ireland and the lifestyle here.

I deeply enjoyed mathematics and problem solving from an early age and hence I chose to pursue engineering.

However for some reason it never sat right with me.

I thought that I had chosen the wrong career and for the last few months I contemplated on what I really wanted to do.

I decided to try coding seeing the popularity of it.

I found out that I really enjoyed coding and saw myself studying this further.

This is how I came about making this basic site. Thanks for reading.
