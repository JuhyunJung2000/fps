Piano

About three summers ago my mother gifted me with a digitial piano.

Sure I loved the sound of the piano but I never saw myself playing it.

I tried to learn some chords and songs in appreciation of the gift.

Later I came to face some hard times, it was then I spent hours at the piano to forget about the present and lose myself for a moment.

I never knew I had improved so much as I only played for myself.

Later I came to play for my church and even teach others.
